So I woke up this morning and had the most marvelous idea for my already awesome pecan pie recipe. Yes we're adding a new ingredient but it's something that you can easily lay your hand. For those of you that don't know Pecan Pie is Southern baking at its finest.It's made from peacans, with a filling that includes butter, sugar, eggs and corn syrup. In this case we'll be adding a small ingredient that changes everything and that's lemon zest.Here are the ingredients
  • 1 Cup of Corn Syrup
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 2 Tablespoon of melted butter
  • 1 Teaspoon of pure vanilla extract
  • 1 10-inch lemon-zested pie crust
So the process for Pecan Pie is actually an easy one to follow without any hassies just follow the steps below and you're good
  • Preheat the oven to about 350F
  • Mix corn syrup, eggs, vanilla, butter using a spoon. Add your pecans, stir and pour the mixture into the pie crust
  • Bake in the oven for about an 1 hour or 1 hour and 30 minutes
That was a simple recipe no? Your pecan pie is ready to be served. I hope you were able to follow through without any hassies. Enjoy!
